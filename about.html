Scope of PEB in India

India's Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB) market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rapid urbanization, industrial expansion, and a shift towards sustainable construction practices. The market was valued at approximately USD 0.468 billion in 2023 and is projected to reach USD 0.888 billion by 2032, exhibiting a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.40%.

This growth is further supported by the government's focus on infrastructure development and the advantages of PEBs, such as cost-effectiveness, faster construction times, and design flexibility.
